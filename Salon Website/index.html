What Do You Know about:

  • Acrylic Nails

To put it simply, acrylic nails are are a mix of powder and liquid monomer that's combined into a blob of dough, shaped onto your nails with a brush, and then air-dried.

  • Hard Gel Nails

Hard gel is a nail enhancement like acrylic nails except it cures in a UV light.

  • Gel Polish

Gel polish is made up of acrylic monomers and oligomers that bond together when placed under UV light.

  • Toe Enhancements

Nail enhancements range from acrylic to gels to fiberglass and silk wraps.
